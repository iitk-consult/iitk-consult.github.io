Predicting the Evolution of Deforestation in a Spatial Zone

With funding from Google AI, we partnered with Moja Global and Makerere University to predict deforestation's evolution in a spatial zone. To achieve this, we used machine learning techniques such as time series forecasting and Long Short Term Memory (LSTM) on satellite images and ground reports collected by Makerere University.
